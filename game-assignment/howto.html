How to play:

Dollar bill will pop out of nowhere on the screen,

one by one.

Simply, click the money to get it.

Once the user clicks the money, it'll disappear

and another dollar bill will show up.

Be fast as the timer will run out.

The game will have three levels:

Level one - Collect $5 in 5 seconds

Level two - Collect $10 in 10 seconds

Level three - Collect $15 in 15 seconds
