How to play:

Dollar bills will pop out of nowhere on the screen, one by one.
Simply, click the money to get it. Once the user clicks
the money, it'll disappear and another dollar bill will show up.
Be fast as the timer will run out.






Level one - Collect $5 in 5 seconds
Level two - Collect $10 in 10 seconds
Level three - Collect $15 in 15 seconds
